Understanding RAG Metrics

Retrieval Hit Rate

Percentage of times the vector database successfully fetches the correct paper for the question. When data is dropped or titles are modified, this hit rate falls.

Mean Token F1 Score

Measures the precise word overlap between the agent's response and the ground truth. Blanking summaries or adding noise destroys text structure, dropping F1.

Judge Accuracy & Score

Evaluated using the local LLM as a judge. It verifies semantic correctness (substance over exact syntax). Noise or missing summaries cause hallucinations and lower scores.

Quality & Freshness Checks

Aggregated assertions checking database constraints (uniqueness of IDs, minimum text lengths) and publication age limits (identifying stale documents).
